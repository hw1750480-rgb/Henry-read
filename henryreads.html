Our Story

Built by a Writer, For Every Reader & Writer

Henry Reads was born from a simple but powerful belief: every story deserves to be told, and every reader deserves to be lost in a great one. Founded by Henry Williams — a professional writer and storyteller — this platform is home to bold voices, captivating worlds, and stories that stay with you long after the last chapter.

We built Henry Reads for the romance lover who stays up until 3am, for the thriller fan who can't put it down, and for the writer who has a story burning inside them — waiting to find its readers.
